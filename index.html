Eliora Hansonbrook

Eliora Hansonbrook is a multidisciplinary intellectual with research interests spanning political science, computer science, and more. Her writing has been published in The New York Times and The Hill, and she translated historical documents for the recent book publication The Einstein of Sex by Daniel Brook. Eliora graduated magna cum laude from Macalester College in 2025 with majors in political science and computer science. She is currently a masters candidate in the Committee on International Relations at the University of Chicago.
